    <h2>About Maktab/Madarsa Furquaniah Trust & Madarsa Furquaniah Society:</h2>
    <p>We are dedicated NGOs committed to uplifting the underprivileged and minorities by providing essential social, educational, and digital skills. Our journey began with Madarsa Furquaniah Society in 1988-89, and since then, we have been steadfast support systems for the community. Now, we operate as Maktab/Madarsa Furquaniah Trust.</p>
